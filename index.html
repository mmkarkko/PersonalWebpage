About me

Ever since I was little, I have gravitated towards computers and technology. With a Bachelor of Science degree in Economics and Business Administration, I've discovered a deep passion for front-end development, where I aim to specialize and contribute meaningfully.

My strongest skills lie in HTML and CSS, complemented by a solid foundation in Java and C#. I tackle challenges with a problem-solving mindset and excel in communication both in Finnish and English. Valuing inclusivity and continuous self-improvement, I'm excited to contribute my diverse skill set to collaborative teams, aiming to drive impactful outcomes in web development.
